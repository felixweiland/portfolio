NFT Generator Project

This was an image generator project that I created for creative friend of mine who was interested in NFT She had just started out and was eager to create a big collection of images. I came up with the idea of making a image generator based on layers, that would combine them into unique pictures - as well as generating metadata for the smart contract and properties.
